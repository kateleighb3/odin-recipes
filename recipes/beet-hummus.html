Beet Hummus

This is an awesome beet hummus recipe.
Excellent taste and texture

Ingredients

  • 1 beet
  • 1/2 head of cauliflower
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 lemon
  • EVOO
  • garlic
  • 1/4 C Tahini
  • 1 can Chickpeas
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Peel beets and carrots. Roughly chop beets, carrots, and cauliflower.
    Place on a baking sheet with a drizzle of EVOO and salt and pepper.
    Bake in the oven at 425 for 30 minutes.
  2. When veggies are done roasting throw them in a blender with chickpeas,
    lemon juice, EVOO, Tahini, garlic, salt and pepper. Blend them all together.
  3. Enjoy!
